Project Details

Low-Cost Educational Mini-Rocket Kit

Description

This project involved designing and assembling a low-cost educational mini-rocket kit aimed at promoting interest in space among students. The kit is used in public schools to provide hands-on learning experiences related to aerospace engineering and rocketry.

  • Context: Educational outreach project.
  • Local: Santa Maria, Brazil
  • Duration: 4 months
  • Team Size: 3 person
  • Timeline: Mar 2020 - Jul 2020

Technologies Used

  • Tools: Latex
  • Concepts: Outreach Project

Tasks

  • Designed and assembled a mini-rocket kit for educational purposes.
  • Presented the kit in public schools to promote hands-on learning experiences.
  • Conducted initial research and secured resources for project implementation.
