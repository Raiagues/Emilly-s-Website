Project Details

Flight Control Researcher

Description

This research project focused on developing advanced flight control algorithms to enhance satellite tracking accuracy in formation flight. The objective was to model dynamic equations and design control systems that would optimize trajectory control while minimizing measurement noise.

  • Context: Research project during the third year of Aerospace Engineering studies.
  • Company: Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica (ITA)
  • Local: São José dos Campos, Brazil (remote)
  • Duration: 1 year
  • Team Size: Individual research
  • Timeline: Sep 2022 - Aug 2023

Technologies Used

  • Programming Languages: MATLAB
  • Tools: Simulink
  • Concepts: Flight Dynamics, Control Systems, Kalman Filtering

Results and Conclusions

  • Developed Extended Kalman Filter (EKF) algorithms in MATLAB to improve satellite tracking accuracy for formation flight, reducing measurement noise by 81.7%.
  • Modeled linear and non-linear dynamic equations for satellite flight dynamics using MATLAB to simulate formation flight scenarios.
  • Designed Linear Quadratic Regulator (LQR) and servomechanism with state feedback controllers in MATLAB for satellite trajectory control.
  • Implemented control systems in Simulink to optimize attitude control and avoid actuator saturation.
